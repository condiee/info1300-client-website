Fanclub Collective is a student-run organization at Cornell University that organizes concerts promoting local and DIY artists. We aim to diversify the music and culture scenes at Cornell by fostering an inclusive and creative space for marginalized communities and artists. We're a not-for-profit organization with about ten active members and several more part-time participating students who help organize the intimate shows we put on in Ithaca throughout the semester.
